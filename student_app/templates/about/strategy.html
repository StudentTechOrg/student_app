Our strategy

In 2017, we re-examined our mission, vision, values and plans against the background of significant changes in higher education. Across the University we asked ourselves, fundamental questions about our organisation, its impact and the way we work. From that our Transforming Lives strategy was created.


In 2020, with a global pandemic and a fundamental set of challenges and opportunities for universities, we started to look ahead at how we need to adapt to be ready for the future.


Our Transforming Lives strategy is now driven by a new set of values and priorities which recognise the impact of the pandemic on Sheffield Hallam - and society at large. These set out our beliefs, philosophies and principles, alongside our objectives to succeed, and provide a clear direction for us to continue our journey to becoming the worldâ€™s leading applied university.

Our mission

We transform lives. We shape our students' futures, preparing them for whatever they choose to do, and create knowledge that provides practical solutions to real world challenges.

Our vision

We will be the world's leading applied university, achieving outstanding outcomes for our students and society, showing the world what a university genuinely focused on transforming lives can achieve.

Our strategy is delivered through
three core activities

Research and innovation

Our research and our industry partnerships provide innovative, practical solutions to real challenges.

Teaching and learning

Our students are confident, creative, resilient and responsible - prepared for whatever they decide to do.

Commitment to place

We are a beacon for what a university can do for and with its community.
